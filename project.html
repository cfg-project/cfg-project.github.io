French Toast with Banana and Blueberries

Do you have all the ingredients?

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Banana
  • Hand full of blueberries
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 tbsp milk
  • 1 tspcaster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 brioche slices thick
  • butter for frying

  • Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract to the sugar. Combine until the sugar has dissolved
  • Dip the brioche bread in the egg mix. Be sure to cover both sides so that it is evenly coated
  • Add butter to the frying pan
  • Carefully place the coated bread into the frying pan to cook until golden brown on both sides
  • Once cooked, place one slice on your plate, drizzle lightly with honey and sprinkle with a few slices of banana and a few blueberries
  • Place the second slice on top and repeat with honey, banana, and blueberries.
  • Place the final slice on top and Finish with a dusting of icing sugar Why not add some excitement to your French toast? Here are some extra toppings and finishes you can add to your French toast: Fruit Compote Dusting of cinnamon Warmed salted caramel Edible flowers Shaved white chocolate

Don’t forget to share your completed dish with us on our social media channels.

Enjoy!
